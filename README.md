# Sales-Performance-Analysis
Sales performance analysis using python, SQL, Power BI

# Problem Statement
A major retail company aims to gain deeper insights into its customers’ shopping behavior to enhance sales performance, customer satisfaction, and long-term loyalty. Recently, the management team has observed shifts in purchasing patterns across different customer demographics, product categories, and sales channels (online and offline). They want to understand the key factors—such as discounts, customer reviews, seasonal trends, and payment preferences—that influence purchasing decisions and encourage repeat purchases.

The objective of this analysis is to explore the company’s consumer behavior dataset to identify meaningful trends and patterns. These insights will help the company strengthen customer engagement and make informed decisions to optimize its marketing strategies, product offerings, and overall business performance.
